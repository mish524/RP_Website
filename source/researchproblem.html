Research Problem

Today, the health sector has become an integral part of our lives. As technology advances, it is being applied to a variety of sectors, making services more efficient.
When a patient admitted to a hospital a bed head ticket is issued. It covers the process from administration to discharge. The bed head ticket holds everything including doctor notes, nurse notes, monitoring charts.
But the main problem at present in Sri Lanka is the lack of an effective mechanism to keep this. Currently in Sri Lanka this process takes place manually. There are many disadvantages to this method of recording patient records. These include misplaced records, misprints, and misalignment.
The main purpose of our research is to supply solutions to this problem using technology
