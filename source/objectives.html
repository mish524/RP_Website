Main objective

  • Maintaining medical records for hospitalized patients

Sub Objectives

  • Automating Bed Head Ticket

    Bed head ticket is the medical record of the inward patient in Sri Lankan health system. It records every aspect of patient's illness and the management from time of admission to discharge/transfer/death. From BHT only manage inward patients. This is a legal document and has best evidentiary value in a court. BHT helps to communicate between the attending doctor and who involve in the management, helps to get medical decisions, and can have health statistics of inward patients in Sri Lanka. From automating BHT we can secure patient details, reduce medical errors, and reduce expenses associated with paper medical records.


  • AI Based Healthcare Chatbot System by Using Natural Language Processing

    It is decisive to have an intelligent agent using natural language processing (NLP) that manages user requests and supplies quick access to government hospital information. AI systems ease virtual nursing assistants that can perform a range of tasks from conversing with patients to directing them to the best and effective care unit. Examine the gap analysis with existing data set. AI agents store this information in the virtual nursing aid system to ease patient admission, doctor-patient communication. This we can have immediate access to care, Improve quality of patient care, Accuracy of facility management information ,24/7 availability, Instant response, and consistency in answers


  • Predicting pneumonia through chest x-rays and Identifying critical situation of a patient

    Pneumonia is the most frequent Disease that cause most of the death in worldwide. Also, in current pandemic situation most of the death are caused pneumonia. Most of the time it is hard to find and treat patients. Our primary goals are to diagnose pneumonia at once and to determine if the patient may develop pneumonia in the future.
